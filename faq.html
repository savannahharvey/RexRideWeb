GENERAL FAQs
What is RexRide?
RexRide is a convenient ride-sharing platform designed to connect riders and drivers quickly and safely.
How do I get BYUI student verified?
In your personal profile page, add your byui.edu email to receive a confirmation email. After accepting the confirmation email, your profile will be BYUI student certified!
How do I get paid for driving someone?
Currently, payments are not processed through the platform. Drivers and riders are responsible for coordinating compensation directly and relying on mutual trust.
